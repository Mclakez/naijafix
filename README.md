NaijaFix is a community-driven reporting platform that empowers citizens to report problems in their communities and track their resolution transparently.

From bad roads and broken streetlights to waste issues and public hazards, NaijaFix helps bridge the gap between citizens and responsible authorities.

1. A citizen submits a report with an image and description


2. The admin reviews the report and assigns it to an officer


3. The officer works on the issue and updates progress


4. Once resolved, proof is uploaded and the report is marked Resolved
